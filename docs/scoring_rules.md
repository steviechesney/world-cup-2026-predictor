\# Scoring Rules



\## Competition Format



Players predict the scores for all World Cup 2026 group stage matches.



Predictions are entered into a template workbook and consolidated into the main results sheet.



\---



\## Points System



| Prediction Outcome | Points |

|---|---|

| Correct score and result | 3 |

| Correct result only | 1 |



Examples:



\- Predicted: 2-1

\- Actual: 2-1

\- Points awarded: 3



\---



\- Predicted: 1-0

\- Actual: 2-1

\- Points awarded: 1



\---



\- Predicted: 1-1

\- Actual: 2-1

\- Points awarded: 0



\---



\## Leaderboard Logic



The leaderboard updates automatically based on:

\- total points

\- number of correct scores

\- number of correct results



\---



\## Workbook Workflow



1\. Prediction templates distributed to players

2\. Predictions copied into the Results worksheet

3\. Match scores entered into Scores \& Fixtures

4\. Points calculated automatically

5\. Leaderboard updated dynamically
